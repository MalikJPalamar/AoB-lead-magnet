# LinkedIn Thought Leadership + TEDx Strategy
## 6-Month Plan to Hit Significant Follower Milestone & Land TEDx Speaking Gig

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Goal**: Land TEDx speaking gig in 6 months + significant LinkedIn follower growth

**Strategy**: Build authority through consistent value-driven content, position for TEDx through proven talk topics, engage actively with community

**Target**: 3,000-5,000 new followers in 6 months

---

## PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (MONTHS 1-2)

### Content Pillars
1. Founder Story - Personal journey, why AI agents, lessons learned
2. Contrarian Takes - Push back against AI hype
3. Behind the Scenes - Product building, team culture, real challenges
4. Educational Value - Explainable AI concepts

### Posting Schedule
- **Frequency**: 4-5 posts per week
- **Best Times** (EST):
  - Tuesday 9:00 AM - Thought leadership
  - Wednesday 12:00 PM - Engagement
  - Thursday 9:00 AM - Educational
  - Friday 10:00 AM - Personal stories
  - Sunday 7:00 PM - Community building

### Month 1 Weekly Content Plan

| Day | Post Type | Hook Example |
|-----|-----------|--------------|
| Tue | Origin Story | "I spent 3 years building AI products that failed. Here's what I learned." |
| Wed | Contrarian | "Everyone is excited about AI agents. I'm worried. 90% will fail." |
| Thu | Tool Recs | "These 3 tools changed how we build AI agents (free)" |
| Fri | Reflection | "The biggest AI opportunity no one is talking about" |
| Sun | Engagement | "Reply with your biggest AI challenge - I'll respond to 20" |
