# Alchemy of Breath Lead Magnet Quizzes

## Project Brief
Create two interactive quizzes for lead capture on alchemyofbreath.com:
1. "What's Your Breathwork Style?" - Assessment quiz
2. "What's Your Trauma Healing Path?" - Assessment quiz

Both quizzes should:
- Capture email before showing results
- Provide personalized recommendations
- Integrate with existing Ontraport forms
- Be visually aligned with AOB brand

## Deliverables
- Two fully functional quiz pages
- Email capture integration
- Results pages with personalized paths
- Analytics tracking

## Constraints
- Must integrate with existing Ontraport
- Must match AOB WordPress site design
- Mobile responsive
- Fast loading

## Timeline
2-3 weeks for full implementation
