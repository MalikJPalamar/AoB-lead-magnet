# What's Your Trauma Healing Path?

## Quiz Overview
An assessment to help users understand their trauma healing style and find the most effective approach for their journey.

## Questions

### Q1: How do you currently relate to your trauma?
A) I avoid thinking about it
B) I want to process it but feel stuck
C) I'm actively working through it
D) I've done significant healing work

### Q2: What does healing mean to you?
A) Finding peace and calm
B) Releasing trapped emotions
C) Understanding what happened
D) Transcending the experience

### Q3: How do you handle difficult emotions?
A) I suppress or avoid them
B) I feel them intensely and want to release
C) I analyze and understand them
D) I observe them without judgment

### Q4: What's your relationship with your body?
A) I feel disconnected from it
B) I feel things deeply in my body
C) I want to reconnect with it
D) I have a healthy relationship

### Q5: How do you prefer to process experiences?
A) Slowly and gently
B) Quickly to get it over with
C) With time to reflect
D) Through body-based approaches

### Q6: What support do you prefer?
A) One-on-one guidance
B) Group healing experiences
C) Self-paced learning
D) Combination of approaches

### Q7: What's your comfort level with intensity?
A) Low - I need gentle approaches
B) High - I'm ready to go deep
C) Moderate - gradual is best
D) Flexible - depends on the day

### Q8: What resonates most?
A) Creating safety and stability
B) Releasing and letting go
C) Making sense of the experience
D) Transforming pain into purpose

## Results

### Result A: The Safety Seeker
Your path focuses on creating safety and stabilization.
- Approach: Grounding techniques, nervous system regulation, somatic experiencing
- Recommended: Stabilization programs, gentle breathwork, container work
- Timeline: 3-6 months foundation work

### Result B: The Release Agent
Your path focuses on emotional release and catharsis.
- Approach: Breathwork ceremonies, EMDR basics, somatic release
- Recommended: Intensive breathwork sessions, body-based trauma work
- Timeline: 6-12 months active processing

### Result C: The Understanding Explorer
Your path combines understanding with processing.
- Approach: Psychoeducation, somatic inquiry, meaning-making
- Recommended: Integrated programs, therapy + breathwork
- Timeline: 6-12 months understanding phase

### Result D: The Alchemist
Your path transforms trauma into wisdom and purpose.
- Approach: Shadow work, consciousness exploration, integration
- Recommended: Advanced breathwork, shamanic modalities, retreat work
- Timeline: Ongoing transformation journey
